Career

Roger started his career going to Ithica college for business law. After graduating, he eventually got a job back in Wells, NY as a teacher. He taught business and law as a teacher. Years passed and in 1969, he became the principal of the school. Roger was principal for Wells high school till 1980. In 1980, Roger got a new job working for the Rome central school district in New York as a business manager. He stayed with the Rome central school district till he retired in the early 1990's.
