Recreation

For recreation, Roger enjoyed hunting, snowmobiling, taking the boat out, the Adirondack Mountains in general and just hanging out. Roger and my father used to hunt a lot together. I've been out in the woods with them a few times when they were hunting, but never saw a deer and spent more time messing around with twigs on the ground than anything. I never had the same patience they had to really get into it. I did appreciate all that delicious venison with my eggs for breakfast though!

I did however spend a lot of time snowmobiling with my Grandfather Roger and dad though. We had a ton of fun doing that. We would go all over the Speculator and Lake Pleasant New York backwoods and mountains. Sometimes, we would snowmobile on terrain that wasn't even really a trail. Getting your ride stuck in a puddle of ice water was always an adventure. I always enjoyed going up this one trail that was very challenging to the top of a mountain. Grandpa led me and my wife up it one time and if you go through the slideshow, you can see us enjoying the view.

Also, we would frequently go to family BBQ's in Wells, New York. I would eat all the shrimp if it was on the menu. I'm not sure what Grandpa was up to at those things, I was too busy playing with the other kids and eating shrimp. I'm sure he had a good time though.
