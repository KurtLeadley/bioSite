Welcome

My grandfather Roger Leadley has done a lot in his life. He grew up in the city, but soon found himself living up North in the Adirondack Mountains of New York. He has been a superintendent of a school, quite a hunter, a father, a husband, a grandfather and also managed to build a house by himself, While living in it!

Many of the things he was involved in during his life has been part of my life too. Everything from trying to swing a hammer, to learning how to snowmobile. He and my father were there. Click around and you will find some information about the house he built, his career and also things he did for fun.
